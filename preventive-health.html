FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions patients ask regarding preventive health check-ups.

What does a preventive health check-up include?

A comprehensive check-up includes blood sugar screening, blood pressure monitoring, cholesterol & lipid profile, thyroid screening, heart health assessment, kidney and liver function tests, and personalized wellness guidance.

How often should I get a preventive health check-up?

It's recommended to have a comprehensive health check-up at least once a year. However, frequency may vary based on age, medical history and existing health conditions.

Can preventive health check-ups detect cancer early?

Yes. Routine screenings and early detection tests can help identify certain types of cancer at an early stage when treatment is most effective.
