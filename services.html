Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

Find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about diabetes care and consultations.

How often should diabetic patients visit the doctor?

Depending on blood sugar control, patients are generally advised to visit every 3–6 months for monitoring and treatment adjustments.

Can diabetes be managed without insulin?

Many patients successfully manage diabetes through lifestyle modification and medications, while others may require insulin based on medical evaluation.

Do you provide preventive health check-ups?

Yes. Preventive screenings help identify diabetes, hypertension, heart disease and other metabolic disorders at an early stage.

Is appointment booking required?

Booking an appointment in advance is recommended for a smooth consultation experience and minimal waiting time.
